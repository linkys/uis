Take the first step towards your Canadian Visa

Canada is the desired destination for millions of people seeking a better life in a free, prosperous, multicultural country

Canada actively welcomes immigration from every corner of the globe. In the 12 months July 2015 – July 2016 Canada 's immigration authority, the IRCC, approved over 300,000 applications from individuals and families, business people and skilled professionals, students and refugees.

You can join them.

Register with UIS and we will assign to you an experienced personal immigration account manager to accompany you through the entire application process – and beyond. UIS works exclusively with fully authorized Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultants. These are RCICs of good standing, certified by the Immigration Consultants of Canada Regulatory Council.

This is your guarantee that our Consultants have the expertise and experience to ensure your application fully complies with all the Canadian government's requirements, giving you the best possibility of success.

Don't delay. Start on the path to your new life today.

Simply follow our proven 3 Step process.

Step 1

Register & Evaluate

Register online or by phone and a dedicated UIS Personal Account Manager will collect the necessary details to initiate your immigration process. With over 60 different Immigrant Application programs available, this begins with an evaluation by a registered RCIC to find the right Visa Application pathway for you.

Step 2

Prepare & Optimize

Your UIS Personal Account Manager will guide you through the preparation of all the necessary documents for your relevant Visa application. Your application will be optimized by a RCIC to ensure it provides you with the best opportunity for success.

Step 3

Submit

Once your application is optimized to perfection it will be submitted by an RCIC who will also continue to liaise with the Canadian authorities on your behalf. Once successful, your UIS personal account manager will accompany you on the first exciting steps of your new life. in Canada.
